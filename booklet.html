Booklet's Big Story

Booklet's Big Story is a 3D platformer made in Unreal Engine 5 with a team of 13. As the Lead Programmer of the project, I was responsible for implementing many gameplay mechanics, managing the repository, and more.

To Be Released: May 2025

My Roles:

Lead Programmer:

As a team lead, I was responsible for coordinating with other disciplines about what features were needed and how they should be implemented. Additionally, I made sure I had a solid understanding of how all the games systems work so that I could be a resource for other team members to direct their questions. As a programmer, I was responsible for implementing a wide variety of systems and mechanics. This ranges from player abilities to level specific mechanics to core game systems used throughout the game.

Repository Manager:

Our team used SVN as our version control system. As the repository manager, I was primarily responsible for managing our repository by merging branches at least once a week so that our team could present a build with all of our team's most recent work.
